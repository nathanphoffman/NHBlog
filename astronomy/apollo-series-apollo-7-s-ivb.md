# Apollo Series: Apollo 7 S-IVB

Posted on Wordpress: 2013-03-16

Posted on Medium: 2017-06-09

![](/images/1-TWhHU4nezN2WqxhUNdIwaA.jpeg)

This is a photo of the S-IVB stage from Apollo 7. The stage was designated “S-IVB-205” and the stage is now in orbit around the sun.

Apollo 7 was the first successful manned Apollo flight. The astronauts performed testing in Earth orbit to prepare future Apollo missions for voyages to the moon.
