# 4/2/2013 Archives: Apollo Series: Earth Rise

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*C3so-CYKJy23VhtnXHNhDA.jpeg)

As Apollo 8 became the first manned-craft to orbit the Moon, the astronauts on board became the first human beings to witness the Earth rise over another celestial body. The astronauts took a few photos including [this one](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:NASA-Apollo8-Dec24-Earthrise.jpg) (the more famous photo) and the black and white photo below.

Apollo 8 was the second successful manned Apollo flight, the first manned Saturn V launch, and the first manned flight to leave low Earth orbit. Apollo 8 was originally intended to conduct testing in Earth orbit, but due to the fear that the Soviets would beat the United States to the Moon with a manned N1 mission, NASA flipped Apollo 8 and 9.
