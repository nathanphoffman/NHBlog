A Love Letter to Donkey Kong Bananza

When I first picked up DK I was (best buy story)

Things it does well:

  • Small details like tiny environmental animals scurrying about

  • The layering of the voxels like grass growing on dirt

  • The cleverness of the platforming design

  • The texture, sound, and feeling of every type of terrain

  • The gameplay loop where fossils are often placed near bananas so each lead to one another then if you can’t find any you find chests with maps or find gold which can be used to buy maps and those maps lead back to more fossils and bananas, and repeat.

  • The cuteness and wholesomeness of DK and Pauline

  • The fact the game can be as hard as you want it to be or just about as easy as you want it to be based on how much you rely on transformations and how many bananas and skills you unlock (save for the final bosses which in all honesty should at least pose some kind of challenge). Some speedrunners even try to do it without some of the bananza forms!
