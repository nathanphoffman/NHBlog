I normally wouldn’t want to get into politics on this blog, but I think NASA is a program we all share interest in. NASA has been struggling with budget cuts since 1988, the year I was born! For every year of my life I have lived so far, NASA has lost an average of 0.02% of the federal budget, that is approximately 720 Million Dollars/year for every year I have lived! (*adjusted for inflation) There are many great things that have come out of the space program, let us not let Apollo 17 be our last manned mission to another body, let us not let another 40 years pass us by… This is my personal appeal to you: at least sign this petition to increase their budget back to what it was in 1988.
