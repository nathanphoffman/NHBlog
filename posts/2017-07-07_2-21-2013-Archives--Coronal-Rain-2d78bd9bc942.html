2/21/2013 Archives: Coronal Rain

This incredible footage from the Solar Dynamics Observatory (essentially an advanced telescope in orbit) captured astonishing activity on the sun that has been dubbed “Coronal Rain” This phenomenon is formed by the twisting of strong magnetic fields. In most cases this would result in an ejection of material, but for some reason the magnetic fields did not break or change significantly Instead, they held relatively steady, allowing plasma to travel along the field lines, cool, condense, and fall back to the sun. This footage was originally taken on July 19th, 2013, but it was not until recently that this footage began to circulate.
