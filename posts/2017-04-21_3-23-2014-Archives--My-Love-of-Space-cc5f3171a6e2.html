3/23/2014 Archives: My Love of Space

As you may have noticed, it has been a while since my last photo. I have been spending a lot of my spare time researching information on astronomy and working on two space-related programming projects. I suppose the point I am making is that while I enjoy nature photography, I absolutely love space. I forced myself to keep on the photography route (which I started only two years ago), but it stands as a shadow next to my passion for the Universe. I will therefore be focusing exclusively on space-related topics. I know many of you have followed this blog to see wonderful photos of nature, and for some of you, you will likely stop coming to this site. However, if you stay with me, I can promise that I will post more frequently than ever before and try to focus on entertaining space-content like: space photos, manned-space progress, interesting news stories, popular scifi-movie reviews, and gameplay of games like Kerbal Space Program.
